What is Chidori?

April 7, 2014

Chidori is a technique first developed by Kakashi Hatake in the anime Naruto. Chidori generates a high amount of lightning chakra to the surface of the user's body. Kakashi later teaches his student, Sasuke Uchiha, the Chidori in hopes of preventing Sasuke from going to Orochimaru searching for more power. Both Sasuke and Kakashi later refine this technique, extending it to a medium range attack.

Team Chidori is chosen as the name for a programming project. The group consists of Chris and Sharon. We are currently implementing a calculator that has some useful features for programmers such as binary bit toggle, binary left shift, and binary right shift. We are currently in the phase of implementing our GUI in JavaFX.
